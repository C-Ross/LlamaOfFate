# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to a community built on kindness, respect, and collaboration. We want discussions and contributions to be constructive, professional, and welcoming to everyone who participates.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Treating others with courtesy and respect
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions before escalating
- Giving constructive, specific feedback on ideas and code
- Accepting feedback gracefully and staying open to improvement
- Keeping discussions focused on the project and shared goals
- Helping others learn and contribute effectively

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Personal attacks, insults, or hostile language
- Repeatedly derailing conversations or arguing in bad faith
- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or sustained disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without permission
- Spam, trolling, or unrelated promotional content
- Any conduct that would make collaboration unsafe or unproductive

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Reports of unacceptable behavior can be sent to c.ross.oss+llamaoffate@outlook.com.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. Maintainers will act in good faith to protect participants and the health of the project.

Possible actions may include:

- A private warning
- Temporary suspension from project spaces
- Permanent removal from project spaces
